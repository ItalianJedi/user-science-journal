Video With Subtitles

For this project, we were to get a video from the public domain and adding accessibility with subtitles. For this project, I choose a video on Thanksgiving etiquette.

A link to the original video can be found here: Dining Together https://archive.org/details/DiningTo1951

The video with subtitles Iâ€™ve added can be viewed here:

Link to YouTube: Dining Together https://youtu.be/unc-r74pTGw

I had a lot of fun with this project. I spent about two hours just browsing the archive, watching lots of cool, old videos. I decided to focus on something about Thanksgiving and came across this gem. Since Thanks giving is just around the corner, I thought it would be a good reminder for everyone on how to properly eat at the dinner table. There are a lot of lessons my family needs to learn and I will make them all watch this video before dinner.

There was nothing really challenging about this project. It took a little bit of time to figure out how to add the automatic subtitles. Once I figured it out I spent a good amount of time editing and word/sentence placement. Though it was a 10 minute video there were a lot of pauses between sentences. Some of the sentences sounded odd to me but I tried to reflect what I heard in the audio the best I could.

Overall, I had a fun time doing this project and may do some more later.
